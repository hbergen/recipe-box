# German potato salad

### Ingredients

* Small bag baby yukon gold potatoes
* Butter (a few big pats)
* Garlic mayo (or garlic + mayo) - small amount
* Salt, black pepper
* Red wine / sherry vinegar
* Dill
* Green onion, sliced (mostly the green parts)
* Red onion, diced

### Instructions

1. Boil the potatoes. Cut them into bite sized pieces.
2. Mix the dill and onions and set aside.
3. Add the potatoes to a medium sized bowl with the mayo and butter and stir until butter is melted. Sprinkle with the vinegar. Add the salt and pepper and stir. Add the vegetables.

Serve warm
